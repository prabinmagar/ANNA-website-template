Overview

The Association of Nepalese Alumni from Australia(ANAA) is a non-government, non-political and non-profit organization of professionals who graduated from various universities or educational institutions of Australia.It was established in 1998 (2055 B.S). ANAA is acclaimed for fostering and strengthening cordial and mutually beneficial relationship among its member in Nepal and Australia. ANAA aims to establish long term relationships with like-minded organizations and associations for the benefit of exchange of experience and opportunities created and shared by those associations to its members. ANAA also focuses on imparting knowledge, skill and expertise of the alumni to contribute to the development of different sectors of Nepal.
