61 Years Together

Written by: Ananta Poudyal

10th August 2021

ANAA in collaboration with the Australian Embassy Nepal was able to virtually launch this book 61 Years Together. This book aims to present the readers with perspectives and stories of people whose actions have played a role in bringing the two countries together, and have sown the seeds of success for the future. It is an attempt to highlight different industries and sectors that have been touched and advanced by the cross-pollination of ideas, and professionals who are associated with Nepal-Australia. We hope that this book will give our readers a flavor of the achievements that the two countries should take pride in celebrating.
