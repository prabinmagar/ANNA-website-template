Who are we ?

The Association of Nepalese Alumni from Australia(ANAA) is a non-government, non-political and non-profit organization of professionals who graduated from various universities or educational institutions of Australia.

It was established in 1998 (2055 B.S). ANAA is acclaimed for fostering and strengthening cordial and mutually beneficial relationship among its member in Nepal and Australia. 61 Year Together

10th August 2021 – ANAA in collaboration with the Australian Embassy Nepal was able to virtually launch this book 61 Years Together. This book aims to present the readers with perspectives and stories of people whose actions have played a role in bringing the two countries together, and have sown the seeds of success for the future. It is an attempt to highlight different industries and sectors that have been touched and advanced by the cross-pollination of ideas, and professionals who are associated with Nepal-Australia. We hope that this book will give our readers a flavor of the achievements that the two countries should take pride in celebrating.

Ananta Poudyal
